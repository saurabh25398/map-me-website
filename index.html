Frequently Asked Questions

Why is there a setup fee?

Healthcare LSA verification is the most complex onboarding we do. It involves NPI registration, malpractice insurance certificate review, DUNS verification, background checks for your clinical team, and state licensing. We clear accounts in 3-4 weeks that typically take practices 3-6 months to do themselves. The setup fee covers that intensive, specialized work.

Do you charge a percentage of my ad spend?

No. LSA isn't a traditional media buy where you bid on keywords. Google charges per lead. If we charged a percentage of your lead costs, we'd have a financial incentive to let those costs rise. We charge a flat fee for our expertise so our incentives are perfectly aligned: getting you the highest quality leads at the lowest possible cost.

Why do you require Google Business Profile (GBP) access?

As of recent Google updates, a verified GBP is mandatory to run LSA campaigns. Furthermore, all reviews shown in LSA ads now pull directly from your GBP. The two platforms are completely intertwined, which is why we manage your GBP alongside LSA in our Growth and Elite tiers.
